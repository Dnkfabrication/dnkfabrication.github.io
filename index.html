About DnK

Precision.
Craftsmanship.
Results.

DnK Fabrication is a custom metal shop based in Lubbock, Texas. We work with residential and commercial clients to design and build metalwork that performs as well as it looks.

From laser-cut architectural panels to structural steel and custom signage, we bring precision engineering to every project. We fabricate — and where powder coating is part of the finish, we handle that coordination too.

No templates. No shortcuts. Every piece is built to your spec.

What We Do

Our Services

01
Laser Cutting

Precision fiber laser cutting on steel, aluminum, and stainless. Complex patterns, tight tolerances, clean edges — every time.

02
Custom Metalwork

Structural and decorative fabrication built to your drawings or developed from concept. Gates, frames, panels, supports, and more.

03
Architectural Fencing

Laser-cut modular fence systems with custom infill patterns. Bolt-together design, powder coated finish options, residential and commercial.

04
Custom Signage

Multi-layer cut steel signage for businesses, properties, and landmarks. Designed, fabricated, and installed by DnK.

05
Commercial Projects

Volume fabrication for contractors and developers. Consistent quality at scale. We work from your specs or can help develop them.

06
Residential Work

Custom pieces for your home — railings, art installations, garden features, decorative panels. Built to complement your property.
